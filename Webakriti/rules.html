Rules and Regulations

  • Eligibility +

    Participants must be registered and adhere to the guidelines set forth for eligibility. Teams and individuals should verify their eligibility status before the event.

  • Team Formation +

    Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members. Ensure that all team members are registered and follow the event guidelines.

  • Registration Details +

    Complete the registration form with accurate details. Any discrepancies may lead to disqualification.

  • Idea Submission +

    Submit your project ideas by the specified deadline. Ensure all submissions comply with the theme and rules of the hackathon.

  • Submission Deadlines +

    Adhere to all submission deadlines. Late submissions may not be considered for evaluation.

  • Important Dates +

    Be aware of all key dates including registration, submission deadlines, and event dates. Check the schedule for updates.
